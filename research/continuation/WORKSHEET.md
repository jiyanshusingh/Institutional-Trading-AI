# Continuation Research Worksheet

## Purpose

This worksheet is used to investigate the earliest deterministic market
event that consistently precedes a continuation move.

The objective is **not** to identify BOS.

The objective is to discover the earliest observable event that
distinguishes continuation from reversal.

All observations should be objective and reproducible.

Avoid ICT terminology unless the observed behavior has already been
validated.

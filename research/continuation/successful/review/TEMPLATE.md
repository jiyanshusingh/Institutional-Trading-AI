# Research Question

> **What objective market event established continuation before structural confirmation (BOS)?**

This question should guide every observation.

Do not explain the BOS.

Instead, identify what objectively changed **before** the BOS became possible.

# Review Workflow

Perform the review in the following order.

1. Open the **clean chart**.
2. Record observations without looking at annotations.
3. Form an initial hypothesis.
4. Open the annotated chart.
5. Compare observations against the detected events.
6. Record objective conclusions only.

# Research Principle

Every statement in this review should satisfy the following criteria:

- Based on observable price behavior.
- Reproducible by another reviewer.
- Independent of ICT or SMC terminology.
- Recorded before drawing conclusions.
- Useful for comparison against other market outcomes.

The purpose of this review is **not** to explain the market.

The purpose is to discover objective market primitives that consistently distinguish successful continuation from other structural outcomes.

# Experiment 001

Title:
Institutional BOS Confirmation

Problem:
Current BOS is generated whenever price closes beyond the previous swing.
This can create false break signals after weak pivots or marginal closes.

Hypothesis:
Requiring a body close plus minimum ATR displacement beyond the swing level
will reduce false BOS signals and improve downstream trade quality.

Status:
Planned

Success Metrics:
- Fewer false BOS signals
- Improved win rate
- Improved profit factor
- Reduced drawdown
